About Our Sole Store

Our Sole Store founded in early 2020 by Team7 and located in the city of Canada,Montreal. We work closely with well-known local brands, and therefore the products sold by Soles Store are original brands that have partnered together.

The aim of the Soles Store is that people can get authentic goods from well-known local brands at standard prices and help introduce local brands so that local brands can grow and participate in advancing the fashion industry in Montreal.
